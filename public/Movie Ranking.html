The Best Movies According to Vaibhav

My top 5 movies of all-time.

Inception

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction thriller that blurs the lines between dreams and reality.

Kimi no Na wa (Your Name)

A fantasy romance anime film about two high school students who mysteriously swap bodies and form a deep connection across time and space.

Interstellar

An epic science fiction film about a group of astronauts who embark on a dangerous mission through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet for humanity's survival.

Tumbbad

A dark fantasy horror film set in 19th century India, centered around greed and the supernatural.

The Dark Knight

A superhero action-thriller film, featuring the iconic Batman facing off against the menacing Joker in a battle of good versus evil.
